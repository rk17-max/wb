List of famous cartoon creators

  1. Hanna-Barbera (William Hanna and Joseph Barbera) - Creators of iconic cartoons like *The Flintstones*, *Yogi Bear*, and *The Jetsons*.
  2. Chuck Jones - Famous for his work on *Looney Tunes*, including the creation of characters like Wile E. Coyote and the Road Runner.
  3. Matt Groening - Creator of *The Simpsons* and *Futurama*.
  4. Jim Davis - Creator of *Garfield*.
  5. Stan Lee - Known for his work on Marvel Comics, which led to animated series such as *Spider-Man* and *X-Men*.
  6. John Kricfalusi - Creator of *Ren & Stimpy*.
  7. Genndy Tartakovsky - Known for *Samurai Jack*, *Dexter's Laboratory*, and *The Powerpuff Girls*.
  8. Alan Moore - Writer for *The League of Extraordinary Gentlemen* and some animated works in the DC Universe.
  9. Craig McCracken - Creator of *The Powerpuff Girls* and *Foster's Home for Imaginary Friends*.
  10. Michael Dante DiMartino & Bryan Konietzko - Creators of *Avatar: The Last Airbender* and *The Legend of Korra*.
